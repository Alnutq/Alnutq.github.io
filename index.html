Welcome

Welcome to the Mallu Muslim Club, a vibrant community dedicated to celebrating the rich cultural heritage of Kerala Muslims. Our club brings together people who share an appreciation for Malayalam music, arts, culture, and Islamic traditions from Kerala.

We host regular events, cultural programs, and social gatherings that showcase the unique blend of Malayali and Islamic heritage. Whether you're from Kerala or simply interested in learning more about our culture, we welcome you to join our community.

Our Playlist

Enjoy our curated Spotify playlist featuring a selection of popular Malayalam songs and Islamic devotional music from Kerala. These tracks showcase the melodious traditions and contemporary expressions that make our musical heritage so special.
